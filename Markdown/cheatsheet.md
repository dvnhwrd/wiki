# Markdown
## Cheatsheet

Symbol | Effect
------ | ------
`#` | Heading 1
`##` | Heading 2
... | ...
`######` | Headng 6
`**` | Bold
`*` | Italics
` - ` | Unordered List
`  - ` | Unordered List (sub-topic)
`1. ` | Ordered List
` 1. ` | Sub-topic
Backticks | Code
`~` | Strikethrough
